How it Works

  1. Pick a template (Referral, Loyalty, Advocacy) and define the reward triggers.
  2. Install the widget or embed your own UI. Generate unique links/codes per user.
  3. Connect billing & CRM. Use webhooks to automate reward fulfillment.
  4. Launch in‑app prompts at key moments and track conversions in the dashboard.
